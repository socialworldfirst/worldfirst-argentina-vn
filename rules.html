Prize draw rules: Vietnam

Promotion terms and conditions for the WorldFirst World Card prize draw.

1.What is this promotion about?

What: Eligible WorldFirst Business customers, new or existing, who hold a World Card can enter a prize draw (the Prize Draw), with up to three winners across the participating markets, each receiving one (1) official Argentina national team jersey, hand-signed by the squad including the team captain (the Prize).

Who: Eligible Participants in a Participating Market (see section 6).

When: The promotion runs from 18 June 2026, 00:00 to 17 August 2026, 23:59 Singapore time (the Promotion Period).

Important: Three (3) Prizes are available, each being one signed Argentina national team jersey (signed by the squad, including the captain), awarded to randomly selected winners across the participating markets. The Prize is the jersey only. It does not include event tickets, travel, or related costs.

You must comply with these terms, the World Card Terms and the Business Terms that apply to your WorldFirst account at all times to take part.

2.Eligibility and the required step

To be an Eligible Participant, you must:

  • have a registered business address in a Participating Market (see section 6),
  • be at least 18 years old,
  • be a WorldFirst Business customer in good standing, and
  • hold an active World Card (physical or virtual) on your account.

Required step: hold a World Card (open one if you don't already have it, available to new and existing WorldFirst customers) and reach at least US$1,500 in Eligible Spend on that card during the Promotion Period. Eligible Spend is defined as the accumulative total of payments made with your World Card to suppliers, services or business expenses during the Promotion Period. You receive one entry per Eligible Participant.

No purchase necessary. You can also obtain one entry without making a payment. Contact WorldFirst customer support during the Promotion Period with your registered business name and the last four digits of your World Card, quoting "Argentina Jersey Draw". Limited to one free entry per Eligible Participant.

3.How the Prize Draw works

Entries close at the end of the Promotion Period. The Prize Draw takes place on a single fixed date, 31 August 2026, when we include all Eligible Participants who completed the required step and randomly select the winners from eligible entrants across the participating markets.

If you win, we notify you by email within 48 hours of the draw, to the email registered to your WorldFirst account. You have 48 hours from notification to confirm and accept the Prize. If you do not respond within that time, we randomly select another Eligible Participant.

Decisions on the administration and operation of the promotion, including selection of winners, are final and binding.

4.Getting your Prize if you win

Once you confirm acceptance, WorldFirst arranges for the Prize to be delivered within 30 days. Delivery is within Vietnam; WorldFirst arranges courier delivery to your registered business address.

There is no cash alternative to the Prize. The Prize is not transferable except as we permit.

5.The Prize

Each Prize is one (1) official Argentina national team jersey, hand-signed by members of the Argentina squad, including the team captain, and authenticated by the Argentine Football Association.

No individual player is named in this promotion and no individual-player endorsement is implied. Players are referred to collectively as the squad and the captain. The Prize is the signed jersey only, with no cash alternative.

6.Participating markets

This edition covers Vietnam, open to businesses registered in Vietnam that hold a WorldFirst World Card (new or existing). The promotion also runs in other participating markets, each under its own terms; winners are drawn at random across the participating markets.
